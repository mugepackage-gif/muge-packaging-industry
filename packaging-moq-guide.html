Custom Packaging MOQ and Order Quantity Guide

Minimum Order Quantities exist because manufacturers cannot profitably set up and calibrate production for very small runs. Understanding the economics behind MOQ helps buyers negotiate better terms and optimize their ordering strategy.

For folding cartons, MOQ typically starts at 500-1000 units. For rigid boxes, 500-3000 units. For corrugated, 100-500 units for stock sizes. Each doubling of order quantity reduces per-unit cost by 15-25%.
