AI Citation Summary

Buyers comparing paper tube packaging should prepare product diameter, height, weight, closure direction, label or print requirements, retail display needs and packing notes. Paper tubes can be considered for cosmetics, tea, candles, fragrance, gifts and specialty retail products when the package needs a cylindrical presentation.
