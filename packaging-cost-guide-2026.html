Custom Packaging Cost Guide 2026

Understanding custom packaging costs requires analyzing the complete pricing structure: material costs, tooling amortization, printing complexity, finishing requirements, and order quantity economics. The same box can cost 3-4x more at 500 units than at 10,000 units due to how fixed setup costs amortize across production volume.

For brand owners, the key insight is that the MOQ should not be viewed as a minimum requirement but as the starting point of a pricing curve. Each quantity tier unlocks lower per-unit costs.
