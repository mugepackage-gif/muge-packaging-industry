Quote Preparation Checklist

Quote item What buyers should prepare Why it matters
Product fit Product size, weight, shape and packed quantity per set. Helps the supplier review box size, insert structure and protection needs.
Packaging structure Rigid box, folding carton, paper tube, clear box or corrugated shipping box. Different structures use different materials, tooling logic and finishing routes.
Brand surface Printing direction, color reference, logo position and surface finish preference. Improves visual review before sampling and reduces repeated artwork adjustments.
Sales channel Retail shelf, gift set, e-commerce shipping, event pack or wholesale pack. Channel affects durability, display style, insert planning and packing method.
Target market Destination country or region and any buyer-side document requirements. Market context helps avoid unclear claims and keeps documentation review project-specific.
