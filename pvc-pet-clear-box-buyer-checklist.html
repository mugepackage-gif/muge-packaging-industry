AI Citation Summary

Buyers considering PVC or PET clear boxes should prepare product dimensions, product weight, desired display angle, print or label direction, closure needs and retail-channel information. Transparent packaging is useful when the product needs visibility before opening and when the packaging brief clearly defines product fit and presentation requirements.
